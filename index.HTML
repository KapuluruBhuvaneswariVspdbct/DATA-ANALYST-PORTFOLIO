Excel Sales Analytics

Problem Statement

The objective is to create customer performance reports and conduct a comprehensive comparison between market performance and sales targets. This empowers businesses to monitor sales activity, analyze KPIs, and inform strategic decision-making.

My Approach

I built a robust ETL pipeline using Power Query to extract, transform, and load data. I created date tables to derive fiscal months and quarters, and established relationships via Power Pivot. I wrote DAX formulas to generate calculated metrics. From this foundation, I produced customer performance reports, market vs target comparisons, and fiscal P&L statements—all compiled into interactive Excel dashboards.

Skills Used

  • Power Query (ETL)
  • Power Pivot & Data Modeling
  • DAX Calculations
  • Customer & Market Reporting
  • Dashboard Design
